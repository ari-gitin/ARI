# ARI Product Vision

## Mission

ARI (Artificial Responsive Intelligence) is a simple, affordable, and friendly personal AI companion.

The goal of ARI is to help people in their daily life through natural conversation, voice interaction, reminders, knowledge, and emotional support.

## Vision

Build an AI robot that anyone can own.

- Simple to use.
- Affordable.
- Privacy focused.
- Always helpful.
- Made for everyone.

## Version 1 Goals

- Voice conversation
- Display with facial expressions
- Wi-Fi connectivity
- Local memory
- Cloud AI support
- Expandable hardware

## Long Term Goal

Create an AI companion that becomes a trusted assistant for every home.
